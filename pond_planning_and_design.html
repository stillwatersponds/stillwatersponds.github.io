POND PLANNING AND DESIGN

As in any industry, good planning leads to a process that produces a product that works.

The vast majority of prospective pond owners seriously underestimate what goes into producing a pond that works. Coupled to that, there is a vast amount of “trades people” who say ‘I can build a pond’ that seriously underestimate what goes into producing a pond that works.

80% of all ponds we touch, will have already been spawned by the aforementioned collective.

If you are reading this and haven’t yet built your pond or indeed are on your second or even third time around. Please dedicate a sensible of amount of time to planning and designing your pond…

"To create a pond to work as you expect, in a way that you understand and can manage. That sits beautifully in your landscape and provides a home for all the inhabitants you would like see… Is not an easy undertaking…. and should be treated as such."

"Planning is imperative, a design far more than a good idea… Save time, money, frustration and stress… plan with somebody who really knows about ponds… as life really is too short "
